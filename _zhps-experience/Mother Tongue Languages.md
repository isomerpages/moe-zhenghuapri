# Mother Tongue Languages

Vision
------

Every Zhenghua student is proficient in the use of Mother Tongue Languages.  

Mission
-------

Provide a conducive environment through collaboration, training, basic language skills, academic programmes, cultural appreciation, global exposure, to nurture pupils with morality and achievements.

  

To ignite our students’ passion in the learning of their mother tongue languages, MTL department offers a wide range of programmes, from Mother Tongue Fortnight, Reading Programmes, Learning Journeys, to creating e-Story Books, just to name a few. Our students are given opportunities to participate in various competitions where they have fun and can showcase their talent. Our teachers are dedicated to creating joy and meaning in our students’ learning. Check out our Zhenghua Gardens to find out more on our programmes for the different Mother Tongue Languages!
