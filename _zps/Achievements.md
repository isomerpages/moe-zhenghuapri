**Achievements**
=================

The Indian Dance group performed Parashiva at the SYF Arts Presentation and achieved Certificate of Distinction! Through regular practices, seniors guided juniors and dancers supported one another with constructive feedback. On performance day, they worked closely as a team, helping with costumes and accessories, demonstrating strong teamwork and a shared commitment to excellence!

![](/images/Achievements/Indian_Dance.jpg)

The Chinese Dance group proudly represented the school at the SYF Arts Presentation and achieved Certificate of Distinction! Through focused practices, students strengthened discipline, coordination and cultural understanding. They delivered a graceful and confident performance on stage.  

![](/images/Achievements/Chinese_Dance.jpg)

The Malay Dance group represented the school at the SYF Arts Presentation and achieved Certificate of Distinction! Through dedicated rehearsals, students built resilience, teamwork and appreciation for Malay dance. On performance day, they showcased confidence and poise on stage. 

![](/images/Achievements/Malay_Dance.jpg)

The Choir team represented the school at the SYF Arts Presentation and achieved Certificate of Distinction! Through dedicated rehearsals, students built resilience, teamwork and appreciation for Malay dance. On performance day, they showcased confidence and poise on stage.

![](/images/Achievements/SYF_Choir.jpg)

Auni Iffah 6EE, Iman Lana 6HY and Arfah Zuhrah 6IN of Zhenghua Primary School wins 1st place & Overall Champion at Juara Baca Berita competition 2026 organized jointly by MediaCorp & NLB. This achievement is a testament to the students' hard work and the dedication of all the Malay Language teachers as well as the support from their parents.

![](/images/Achievements/Juara_Baca_Berita.png)

Our Band SYF Team has achieved an Accomplishment at their recent SYF Arts Presentation. This achievement is a testament to the students' hard work and the dedication of their teachers.
