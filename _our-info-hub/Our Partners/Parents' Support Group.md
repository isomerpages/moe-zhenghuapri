<h2>Parents' Support Group</h2>
<p>A strong partnership to develop character and aspirations in our children
to achieve their goal.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>Through quality partnership with the school and active collaboration with
staff and community, we strengthen the holistic development of every child
of Zhenghua to be the best that they can be.</p>
<p><strong>Every Parent A Supportive Partner</strong>
<br>We believe that Parents can be our valuable partners in contributing towards
strengthening the holistic development of our children. Zhenghua Primary
School would like to cordially invite you to be a member of the Parents
Support Group (PSG). We strongly encourage you to play an active role in
our school by becoming a PSG member to partner us in our efforts to develop
our children to be the best that they can be.</p>
